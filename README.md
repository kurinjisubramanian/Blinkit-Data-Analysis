# Blinkit-Data-Analysis- Blinkit Customer Analysis using MYSQL
In this project, we have analysed the various users of blinkit by leveraging customer segment, spending patters, order frequencies, trends etc. 
The objective is to help to analyze BlinkIT customer data using SQL to understand customer demographics, purchasing behavior, customer value, and acquisition trends, enabling data-driven business decisions for customer retention and revenue growth
1. Customer Segment Distribution
Objective:
To identify the distribution of customers across different customer segments and understand which segment forms the largest share of the customer base.

Business Value:
Understand customer composition, 
Support targeted marketing strategies
Identify opportunities to grow premium customer segments

2. Average Order Value by Customer Segment
Objective:
To compare the average spending behavior across different customer segments and determine which segment contributes the highest value per transaction.

Business Value:
Identify high-spending customers
Design personalized promotions
Improve customer profitability

3. Total Orders by Customer Segment
Objective:
To evaluate purchasing frequency across customer segments and identify which groups contribute the highest transaction volumes.

Business Value:
Measure customer engagement
Prioritize retention initiatives
Identify loyal customer segments

4. Top 10 High-Value Customers
Objective:
To identify customers with the highest estimated lifetime value based on their purchase frequency and average order value.

Business Value:
Recognize VIP customers
Develop loyalty programs
Improve customer retention

5. Monthly Customer Registration Trend
Objective:
To analyze customer acquisition trends over time and identify periods of increased or decreased customer registrations.

Business Value:
Evaluate acquisition performance
Measure campaign effectiveness
Identify seasonal trends

6. Area-wise Customer Distribution (Bonus Analysis)
Objective:
To determine the geographic distribution of customers and identify locations with the highest customer concentration.

Business Value:
Optimize delivery coverage
Support expansion planning
Allocate marketing resources effectively

7. Estimated Customer Lifetime Value by Segment (Bonus Analysis)
Objective:
To estimate the long-term value generated by each customer segment using purchase frequency and average order value.

Business Value:
Prioritize valuable customer segments
Improve customer retention strategies
Optimize marketing investments
Overall Business Objective

To leverage SQL-based customer analytics to understand customer behavior, spending patterns, and acquisition trends, enabling BlinkIT to make informed decisions on customer segmentation, marketing strategies, and long-term business growth.
